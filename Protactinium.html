Discovered by : O. Hahn and L. Meitner in Berlin, Germany, and independently by K. Fajans in Karlsruhe, Germany, and Frederick Soddy and co-workers in Glasgow, UK

Year Discovered : 1917

Origin : The name is derived from the Greek ‘protos aktino’, meaning first ray.

Description :
A radioactive metal, small amounts of which are found naturally in uranium ores. It is also extracted in gram amounts from spent fuel rods from nuclear reactors.
