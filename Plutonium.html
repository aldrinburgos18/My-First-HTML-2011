Discovered by : Glenn Seaborg and co-workers

Isolated by Berkeley, California, USA

Year Discovered : 1940

Origin : The element is named after the planet Pluto.

Description :
A man-made, radioactive silvery metal extracted in tonnes from spent nuclear fuel rods. In its turn, it can also be used as a nuclear fuel and in nuclear weapons. It is dangerously radioactive and the 244Pu isotope has a half-life of thousands of years.
