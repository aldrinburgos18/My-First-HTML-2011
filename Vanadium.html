Discovered by : A.M. del Rio

Year Discovered : 1801

Isolated in Mexico City, Mexico

Origin : The element is named after 'Vanadis', the old Norse name for the Scandinavian goddess Freyja.

Description :
A silvery metal that resists corrosion and is used in alloys and in steel, as well as on colour television screens. It is essential to some species, including humans, although we require very little - less than the 0.04 milligrams we take in each day.
